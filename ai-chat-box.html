Meal Plan Suggestions:
Focus on Whole Foods: Incorporate a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins into your meals. Aim for a colorful plate to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients.
High-Antioxidant Foods: Include foods like berries, spinach, kale, broccoli, and carrots, which are rich in antioxidants to support your immune system.
Lean Proteins: Opt for fish, chicken, eggs, legumes, and plant-based proteins like tofu or lentils to support muscle repair and recovery.
Healthy Fats: Add sources of omega-3 fatty acids, such as salmon, walnuts, flaxseeds, and avocados, to help reduce inflammation.
Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Herbal teas and clear broths are also good options.
